Drop your launch soundtrack here as `launch.mp3`.

The /launch page expects exactly this path:
  /public/music/launch.mp3

The "♪ Play sound" button in the top-right of the launch page will pick it up automatically. If the file is missing, the button still renders but does nothing on click.

Recommended sources for an Apple-launch-tonality track:
- Pixabay (free, no attribution): https://pixabay.com/music/search/cinematic%20ambient/
- Bensound (free with attribution): https://www.bensound.com/royalty-free-music/cinematic
- YouTube Audio Library: https://studio.youtube.com/channel/UC/music

What to look for:
- Slow piano + soft pad, OR sparse synth + ambient drone
- BPM 60-80 (slow, deliberate)
- 2-4 minutes long, the audio loops automatically
- No vocals
- No big drops or surprises (keep it ambient)

A good test: if it could underscore an Apple keynote intro, it works.
